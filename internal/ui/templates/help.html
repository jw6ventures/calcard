Help & Getting Started

CalCard hosts your calendars and contacts and syncs them to any CalDAV/CardDAV app. This page walks through everything in the welcome tour — at your own pace, and always here when you need it again.

Start tutorial Reach out to the community

1 Find your way around

Use the top navigation to reach your Dashboard, Calendars, Address Books, Birthdays, and App Passwords at any time. The 🌙 / ☀️ button in the header toggles light and dark mode.

2 Connect a client app

Add this server URL to Apple Calendar, Thunderbird, DAVx5, or any other CalDAV/CardDAV client:

{{.DAVEndpoint}}

Sign in with your email and an app password (created in step 4) — not your login password. Your devices will then sync automatically.

3 Calendars & contacts

Create and manage your collections right here in the web UI:

  • Calendars — add calendars, events, and import/export .ics files.
  • Address Books — keep contacts that sync to your phone and desktop.

Everything you add here shows up in your connected apps, and vice versa.

4 App passwords

Client apps authenticate with app passwords instead of your login credentials, so you can revoke a single device without affecting the others.

Head to App Passwords to create one, then paste it into your calendar/contacts app alongside the server URL. That's it — you're all set!
